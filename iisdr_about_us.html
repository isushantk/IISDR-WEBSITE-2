About IISDR:

The Indian Institute of Skill Development and Research (IISDR) is a dedicated initiative established under the aegis of Utkarsh Charitable and Welfare Trust (UCWT) with the objective of empowering individuals through high-quality skill development, vocational training, and research-driven learning. IISDR is a professional institution was created with a strong belief that skill-based education is the foundation for personal growth, sustainable livelihoods, and national progress.

At IISDR, we focus on providing industry-relevant, practical, and affordable training programs that cater to learners from diverse backgrounds. Our institute works systematically to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skill requirements by designing courses that are aligned with the evolving needs of modern industries.

Backed by the values and commitment of Utkarsh Charitable and Welfare Trust, we strive to make skill education accessible for all—especially for underprivileged and rural communities. The Trust's long-standing work in social welfare, education, women empowerment, and community upliftment forms the backbone of IISDR's mission.

The training methodology at IISDR emphasizes hands-on learning, project-based activities, workshops, and continuous evaluation, ensuring that each learner gains confidence, competence, and the ability to excel professionally. Through our dedicated team of trainers, updated course materials, and structured support system, we aim to nurture skilled professionals and responsible individuals.

IISDR also focus on research and innovation, working to identify skill gaps and introduce new training modules that can contribute to the nation's skill ecosystem. Our commitment extends beyond training; IISDR actively participates in community outreach programs, awareness campaigns, and livelihood initiatives to make a broader social impact.

At IISDR, we believe that every individual deserves an opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed. With the guiding support of Utkarsh Charitable and Welfare Trust, we continue to work toward creating a skilled, empowered, and self-reliant society.
