Our Certification Programmes

Industry-recognized programs designed to build competence, enhance employability, and foster professional excellence.

8 Weeks

Certification Program on New Labour Code

Master India's new Labour Code (2020) including wages, social security, and industrial relations. Designed for HR professionals, legal consultants, and organizational managers to ensure compliance and implementation.

Labour Law Compliance HR
6 Weeks

Train the Trainer (ToT) on Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH)

Equip trainers to conduct POSH awareness programs in organizations. Learn POSH Act 2013, complaint mechanisms, and sensitivity training to create safe and respectful workplaces.

POSH Act Training Workplace Safety

Executive Certification Program on Strategic Human Resources Management

Develop strategic HR capabilities including workforce planning, organizational development, talent management, and business alignment. Ideal for HR leaders and business executives.

HR Strategy Executive Management
