About IISDR

The Indian Institute of Skill Development and Research (IISDR) is a premier institution established by the Utkarsh Charitable and Welfare Trust (UCWT). We are dedicated to advancing skill development and innovative research in alignment with India's vision for human capital excellence. Our programs are designed to meet industry demands while fostering academic rigor and social responsibility.
