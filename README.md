# IISDR Website

## Indian Institute of Skill Development and Research (IISDR)

An official, accessible, and authoritative institutional website for the **Indian Institute of Skill Development and Research (IISDR)**, an initiative of the **Utkarsh Charitable and Welfare Trust (UCWT)**.

### Website Features

- **Professional Government Portal Aesthetic**: Designed following official Indian Government portal design principles (inspired by dpe.gov.in)
- **Accessible & Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly 2-column layout that adapts to all screen sizes
- **Comprehensive Navigation**: Full navigation with dropdown menus for MoU Partners and E-Journal sections
- **Professional Color Scheme**: Navy Blue (#003d99), Saffron (#ff9933), and Green (#138808) accents

### Pages Included

1. **Homepage** (`iisdr_website.html`)
   - Hero carousel with image slider
   - 3-column content grid
   - Announcements ticker
   - Social media section

2. **About Us** (`iisdr_about_us.html`)
   - Comprehensive IISDR information
   - 2-column layout with sidebar
   - Categories and tags
   - Recent news

3. **Programs** (`iisdr_programs.html`)
   - Certification programs listing
   - 10 program items with details
   - Sidebar widgets

4. **Activities** (`iisdr_activities.html`)
   - Community activities and events
   - 10 activity listings
   - Same layout as Programs page

5. **E-Journal** (`iisdr_ejournal_about_thrive.html`)
   - THRIVE journal information
   - Publication details
   - Sidebar with categories and tags

6. **Journal Particulars** (`iisdr_journal_particulars.html`)
   - Journal metadata and details
   - ISSN, Publisher, Chief Editor information
   - Contact details and website links

7. **Editorial Board** (`iisdr_editorial_board.html`)
   - Chief Editor information
   - Editorial Board members (6 members)
   - Advisory Board members (4 members)
   - Contact details and institutional links

### Navigation Features

- **MoU Partner Dropdown**: 5 partner organizations
  - Infosys Limited
  - TCS (Tata Consultancy Services)
  - MSNIM - Manel Srinivasa Nayak Institute
  - Department of Hospital Administration
  - Utkarsh Charitable & Welfare Trust

- **E-Journal Dropdown**: 7 options
  - About THRIVE
  - Journal Particulars
  - Editorial Board
  - Author Guidelines
  - E-Journal
  - Disclaimer
  - Contact Us

### Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Primary Navy Blue: #003d99
- Accent Saffron: #ff9933
- Green: #138808
- White: #ffffff
- Dark: #1a1a1a

**Typography:**
- Font: Arial, sans-serif
- Professional, highly readable
- Optimized for government portal aesthetics

**Layout:**
- 2-column responsive grid (main content + sidebar)
- Switches to 1-column on smaller screens (1024px and 768px breakpoints)
- Professional borders and subtle shadows
